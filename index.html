Übersicht Widgets submit your own

A list of community widgets for Übersicht. Please make sure to read the disclaimer before you download a widget here. To install a widget, just drop the widget, called something like widget-name.widget, into you widgets folder. You can access your widgets folder by selecting 'Open Widgets Folder' from the Übersicht menu.
